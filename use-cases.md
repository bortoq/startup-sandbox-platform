# Use Cases

## Solo Founder

A solo founder has a rough idea but no structure. The platform creates a project brief, roadmap, landing page, and validation tasks.

## Indie Hacker Community

A community submits ideas, votes on them, funds small tests, and helps contributors join the most promising projects.

## Venture Studio

A studio uses the platform to turn many raw concepts into comparable project spaces with market notes and execution plans.

## University Or Hackathon

Students submit ideas and get automatic project scaffolds, public pages, feedback forms, and task lists.

## Open Innovation Program

A company or city collects problems from users and turns the best ones into funded experiments.

## Open-Source Project Incubator

Maintainers publish project ideas, collect contributors, attach bounties, and track progress.
